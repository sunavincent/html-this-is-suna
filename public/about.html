About me

Hi, I’m Suna—a 37-year-old career changer with a journey full of twists and turns. Born in China and educated in Denmark in Multimedia Design and E-Concept Communication, I unexpectedly found my passion in the food and beverage industry, which became my career for nearly 15 years across three countries.

In 2023, a health setback prompted me to explore new opportunities, and I rediscovered coding—a spark from my past studies. With renewed passion and clear goals, I’m now on a mission to build a future in web development. I’m honing my skills in HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, creating user- friendly websites while preparing for a Web Developer training program starting February 2025.

My goal is to design impactful, functional websites and craft a portfolio that opens doors to exciting new opportunities. Let’s create something amazing together!
